About

What is TigerHacks?

TigerHacks is a hackathon hosted at the University of Missouri in Columbia Oct. 13-15 for students of all backgrounds with a passion for technology, design, and, most importantly, building things. TigerHacks brings together hundreds of students from around the country for 48 hours of collaboration, problem solving, and building. We will provide a unique opportunity for students to work with resources and companies they cannot find anywhere else. [[Short blurb about the theme]]
